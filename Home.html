Who we are?

CCSI Africa is a Kenya-based capacity-building advisory and training firm focused on national and international security and conflict resolution issues. The enterprise was formed in 2020 as a subsidiary of the New York City headquartered CCSI. Establishment of CCSIAfrica is largely motivated by addressing unique challenges on the continent with the most appropriate expertise drawn from a magnificent pool of experts from the continent and all over the world. We are here to provide solutions
