About Us

Head of office

CCSI Africa is led by Thomas W Bifwoli, a Senior International development and Border Control expert, He worked with the Kenya Revenue Authority as a senior manager. He has also served for more than 8 years as coordinator and border control expert of UN sanctions monitoring groups, and as chief of the World Customs Organization Regional Intelligence Liaison Office for Eastern and Southern Africa (WCO â€“ RILO ESA) Mr. Bifwoli has consulted for the WCO leading training and field operations in among others Nigeria, Ghana, Tanzania, Kenya, and Namibia. He has also extensive experience training law enforcement officers in Border control best practice, Risk management, post clearance audit, one stop border post, and economic integration, among others in many countries. He has also consulted for the IMF Mr. Bifwoli holds a Masters degree in Public Policy of the Strathmore Business School, Strathmore University. When not doing this, He is a duathlete having qualified to represent his country in the world multisport championships.
